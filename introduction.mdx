## what is jugg?  

**jugg** is more than just a bot — it’s your server’s new best friend. packed with powerful moderation, seamless automation, and next-level customization, jugg helps you **run your server your way**.  

### why jugg?  

- **advanced antinuke** — keep your server protected at all times  
- **moderation that works** — ban, mute, warn, and track members effortlessly  
- **custom automations** — set up roles, reactions, and triggers for smooth management  
- **next-gen music system** — high-quality audio with spotify and last.fm integration  
- **fun, memes & reactions** — keep your server engaged with tons of entertainment commands  
- **deep integrations** — connect jugg with social media, fortnite item shop, and more
